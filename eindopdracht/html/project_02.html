Amsterdam Dance Event x Soundcloud - Branding/UX design

“A New interpretation of waiting”

Amsterdam Dance Event is an enormous event that attracts thousands of visitors. With such an amount of visitors comes long queuing times. The mockups below display a digital service that make an attempt at making these waiting lines shorter and more user friendly.

Note: these designs were commissioned by Amsterdam University of Applied Science. Amsterdam Dance Event and Soundcloud were not involved in this project.
